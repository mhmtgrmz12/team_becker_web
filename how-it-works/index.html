How it works

Four calm steps

No login, no dashboard. Just a calm conversation and a clear finish line.

1

Message us

Open WhatsApp or the chatbot, tell us what you need. We'll send back the exact list of documents for your vehicle type.

2

We pre-verify

Send photos of your documents. We review them digitally and flag anything missing before your trip to us — no wasted visits.

3

Hand over the paperwork

Stop by our office at Bayernstraße 90, drop documents at the secure lockbox, or request a courier pickup. We take it from there.

4

Plates in your hand

We physically process your application at the Rheine authority. Stamped documents and new plates ready within 48 hours — pickup or courier.
